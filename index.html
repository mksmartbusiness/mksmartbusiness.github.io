About Me

The calm
in the chaos.

Hi, I’m Megan the founder of MK Smart Business Solutions and your behind-the-scenes secret weapon.

With almost two decades of experience spanning pharmacy operations, health sector administration, financial administration, learning and development and stakeholder engagement, I bring real depth across the full back-end of a business. I’ve coordinated large-scale programs, managed budgets to the cent, designed and delivered wellbeing initiatives and kept complex projects and programs running smoothly behind the scenes.

I genuinely love helping purpose-driven business owners bring ideas to life and I have a knack for taking a half-formed idea that’s still finding its shape and turning it into something real. You don’t need to have it all figured out before you come to me. That’s kind of the whole point.

As your Online Business Manager, I keep your projects moving, your systems running and your operations on track because working smarter, not harder, starts with having the right person handling the details. You focus on the work that matters most, with someone in your corner who’s as invested in your success as you are.

Where a Virtual Assistant handles tasks, an OBM takes ownership. I quietly make the magic happen behind the scenes, managing the moving parts, keeping the systems humming and creating the operational clarity that lets you stay focused on what you do best.

What I Do

Six pillars.

Full coverage.

Genuine depth across six service areas, built from real experience managing complex programs, systems, budgets and stakeholders.

01
Administration & Executive Support

Email, calendars, meeting facilitation, action registers, records management, procurement and policy & procedure drafting. Nothing falls through the cracks.

02
Project & Operations Management

End-to-end project coordination, workload planning, risk tracking, launch management, client onboarding and status reporting. All handled with clear communication at every stage.

03
Financial Administration

Budget and expenditure tracking, invoice management, payment tracking, timesheet and payroll administration support. Advanced Excel connected workbooks, dashboards and budget models that actually make sense.

04
Communications & Content

Internal communications, newsletters, announcements, content review, social media scheduling, campaign coordination and website accessibility reviews. Clear, consistent and on-brand every time.

05
Design & Visual Communications

Canva asset creation across social, digital and print. Course banners, LMS visuals, internal comms design, branded templates and brand kit management. Assets that look like they belong.

06
Learning, Development & Events

Wellbeing, leadership and staff development program design and coordination, full event and workshop logistics, facilitator management, online membership platform management, LMS administration and e-learning creation. Because when your people thrive, your business does too.
